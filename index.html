Registered seat of a Slovak company in Bratislava

Every company is required to be registered in the Slovak Business Register with its own registered address. Setting up a registered office address for your business is important for ensuring your company's relationship with the public and authorities, for the purpose of entering your company into the Commercial or Small Business Register, and for the purpose of tax registration and for the Trade Licensing Office.

For just 12 Euro/month, we are offering a registered seat and virtual office of your new or existing company at our location in the center of Bratislava on the Obchodna 60 street. Our registered office services are provided for companies in accordance with the Slovak legal conditions, and are suitable for foreign or local businessmen without requiring prior extent knowledge of the Slovak law and regulations. Our services enable your business to be prominently presented without requiring rental of physical office space.

  • Catch a bargain

    For just 12 Eur/month the Obchodna 60 address can be the registration address of your business or your company in Slovakia.

  • Excellent location

    Our shopping mall passage on the Obchodna 60 street is located in the heart of Bratislava, which makes the process of registering the address of your new business in Slovakia extremely convenient.

  • Immediate forwarding of received correspondence

    Whenever there is new mail coming to the mailbox you will be notified about it.

  • Contract length

    Minimum term of contract for a registration address is one year.
